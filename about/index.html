Wolf, Imanol, Hirano in Hovsgol Aimag, Mongolia

Welcome to my DEN! Here is where I scribble about my hobbies.

I like traveling, be it a long tour in northern Mongolia’s steppe on a bicycle saddle or a spiritual tour across time into the history, nature and literature of another culture. I believe it is possible to cross the barriers of language, culture, stereotype, misinformation, or even time and space, to achieve true understanding and empathy.

I studied journalism & communication in university, and had worked as journalist, editor and translator in Beijing, Shanghai, Wuhan and Guangzhou.

If you are into ancient Chinese poetry, or cycling tours in China, hopefully some posts here could be of use. I still haven’t figure out how to set up a seamless and functional comment system. Work in progress. Please bear with me.
