Old Quarters in Xiangyang Revisited

Bai Juyi 白居易 (772-846)

Last time in Xiangyang,
I was growing my first mustache.
Here I am, visiting Xiangyang again,
My mustache and sidelocks almost grey.

Past journeys are but dreams.
Back at this estranged corner, I found “return” a word hard to say.
Hermit’s hut in the eastern city where I used to stay,
Who is now enjoying its long decay?

Old acquaintances, mostly gone.
Old streets, all beyond recognition.
Only you remained, River Han,
Your autumn waves and mists remained the same as the old days.

再到襄陽訪問舊居


昔到襄陽日，髯髯初有髭。

今過襄陽日，髭鬢半成絲。

舊遊都是夢，乍到忽如歸。

東郭蓬蒿宅，荒涼今屬誰。

故知多零落，閭井亦遷移。

獨有秋江水，煙波似舊時。




Translation dedicated to the old Jiulongjing 九龍井 neighbourhood, Wuchang
