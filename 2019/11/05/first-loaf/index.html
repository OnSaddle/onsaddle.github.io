First Loaf in 2019 今年開爐第一包

Author: Wolf Date: November 5, 2019  16:06:24 Category: WhatsCooking

After a year without oven and the smell of parched flour, the baking hibernation is finally over.

An ideal oven should be a low-tech appliance. Mine is a robust mechanically controlled 38L Dolim, freshly arrived from Foshan.

I decide to get warmed up with a rustic, nordic style loaf. The warm, slightly sour smell of rye did wake up all the memory of the coldest days in Guangzhou that year when making bread in a tiny 15L oven saved my life.


dough


baking


cooling


magic of Maillard


caramelised


crumb structure

I can gaze at bread or dough for hours. It’s a living thing. A dough is very much like a baby.

Baking is kind of a reminder. If you can bake, you can love.
