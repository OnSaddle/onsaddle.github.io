The Call of The Campfire (Lockdown Version) 篝火的呼唤（封城版）

Author: Wolf Date: March 17, 2020  16:06:24 Category: WhatsCooking

Spring has come and it is just wrong to miss the hiking season like a prisoner.

All I can do is to use my DIY wood stove to grill some sausages and pretend I’m sitting in the wood.

Without imagination the human life would be too ridiculous to swallow down like stale bread.
