Love and Let Live 愛的進化

Author: Wolf Date: July 11, 2020  16:06:24 Category: skiff


To wake up to the

​ cooing of Lace-necked Doves（Spilopelia Chinensis）

I made humble dedication

​ of wheat grain, sunflower seed, and a screened window ledge


After days of delibration, they accepted the invitation

​ Now we neighbours are getting along well

I served some naan crumbles

​ with just amazing fragrance, hoping they could dine with me closely


Then they came, saw me, and backed away


They and Oriental Turtle Doves (Streptopelia Orientalis)

​ my mid-sized avian friends enjoy feasting grains

But they only visit with no zealous hosts in sight

​ and to maintain our relationship we need both good intention and a comforting distance


Living with my folks is a torturous business

​ Always too much good intention, and never enough distance

I know I chose the Hard Way, maybe wrong way even

​ But deep inside I do hear the call of Karma


Born in the 40s, when war and revolution devastated villages

​ My folks belong to the last generation of pre-modern rural traditions

They struggled, complied, survived and settled down

​ not in villages any more - in the Big Wuhan by the Yangtze


Farewell, hardship in the rice paddy and ponds

​ Farewell, big families and benevolent aunties

They became city folks with mud barely dry on legs

​ A handicraft worker and an SOE propagandist ~


Fate offered terms under which

​ they managed to make ends meet, raising children and providing for my grandma

Be the era of revolution or reform

​ “family” is all they know


Education, individualism, personal freedom and Sting’s song

​ (If You Love Somebody, Set Them Free)

All are luxuries they heard about none

​ and all are luxuries I took for granted


These two old folks

​ My poor, old , life-long-over-bullied-and-brainwahsed parents

They deserve something, for their life,

​ something other than an empty nest in their most fragile days


My existence so far has borne little significance for other beings

​ - A fact I both cherish and lament in dreams

A voice said, “Go back to them and see them off.

Then you would earn your right to be free - real free ”

It’s not a witty voice but I heard it

​ I have been loyal to that voice all my life

If it is the right thing to do, fine

​ If it is wrong? All right then, no regret
