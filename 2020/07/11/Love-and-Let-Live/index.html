Love and Let Live 愛的進化

Author: Wolf Date: July 11, 2020  16:06:24 Category: skiff



To wake up to the

​ cooing of Lace-necked Doves（Spilopelia Chinensis）

I made humble dedication

​ of wheat grain, sunflower seed, and a screened window ledge


After days of delibration, they accepted the invitation

​ Now we neighbours are getting along well

I served some naan crumbles

​ with just amazing fragrance, hoping they could dine with me closely


Then they came, saw me, and backed away


They and Oriental Turtle Doves (Streptopelia Orientalis)

​ my mid-sized avian friends all enjoy feasting grains

But they only visit with no zealous hosts in sight

​ To maintain our relationship we need both good intention and a reassuring distance
